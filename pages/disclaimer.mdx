# I Love You

To my girlfriend, I love you.<br />
I love the way you smile,<br />
the way you laugh,<br />
the way you look at me.<br />
I love the way you make me feel. <br />
I love you for who you are,<br />
for who you were,<br />
and for who you will be.

I will always love you. <br />
I will always be there for you. <br />
I will always be your best friend. <br />
I will always be your lover. <br />
I will always be your everything.
